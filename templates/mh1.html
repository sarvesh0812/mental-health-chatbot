MENTAL HEALTH AWARENESS

Mental health is just as important as physical health. It affects how we think, feel, and behave in daily life. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.

Taking care of your mental health through mindfulness, therapy, and self-care is essential for a balanced, fulfilling life. Remember, it's okay not to be okay, and seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness.

Practice mindfulness and meditation daily
Stay connected with loved ones
Get regular physical exercise
Prioritize quality sleep
